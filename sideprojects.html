Side Projects

Aluminum Foundry

Built a homemade Aluminum foundry capable of melting all variations of aluminum. I have produced multiple Aluminum Ingots weighing in at just under 5 pounds in total. Future plans include perfecting the melting process and casting parts out of the ingots.

Delta 3D Printer

Built a homemade 3D printer. Notable features of the printer include the Altiods heat sink and the hand cut base. Future work is centralized around calibrating the printing process so as prints will look more like the AutoCAD model.
