Bl!nkware
Wright Patterson Air Force Base Discovery Lab - Research Intern

Bl!nkware is another project I worked on will being a research intern for the United States Air Force. The goal of Bl!nkware is to address the problem that fatigue has on drivers. Through the use of an IR sensor and basic Arduino, Bl!nkware alerts drivers to their drowsy condition.


All of the necessary material for the operation of the glasses is contained within the glasses themselves including power and the code that runs the glasses. Bl!nkware fits snugly on the average human head. These glasses represent an initial version of what may soon be a commercial product.
