RCI Robot
Wright Patterson Air Force Base Discovery Lab - Research Intern

Shown below is the RCI (Remotely Controlled Inspection) Robot. This crawler was designed and created to assist civil engineers in the bridge inspection process. RCI implements a Raspberry Pi 2 for not only control of the crawler, but to also transmit live video feed to the user. It does this by turning the Raspberry Pi into a router. This means an inspector can control the RCI Robot with any "smart" device.


It should be noted that this project was presented to numerous Air Force officals. Each one of these officials were skeptical as to whether the RCI Robot would actually be able to crawl along a bridge. It was at this time (when their skepticism was at a maximum) the video below was shown to them, washing away any doubt about its abilities.
